About DMeet

Where Every Local Event Becomes an Adventure!

Our Mission

DMeet bridges the gap between local event organizers and community members, creating vibrant connections through shared experiences. We believe that every neighborhood has amazing events waiting to be discovered, and every person has the potential to create meaningful connections.

What Makes DMeet Special

Location-Based Discovery

Find events happening within walking distance or plan adventures further away with our precise distance filtering.

Make Real Connections

Connect with up to 3 like-minded people at each event. Build your network through shared experiences and interests.

Quality Through Feedback

Rate and review events to help organizers improve. Our feedback system ensures continuously improving event quality.

Easy Event Creation

Share your passion by creating events. Our intuitive interface with interactive maps makes event organization simple.

How DMeet Works

1
Discover Events

Browse local events on our homepage or explore them on an interactive map. Filter by distance, category, and date to find exactly what interests you.

2
RSVP & Attend

Reserve your spot at events that interest you. Get event details, directions, and manage your schedule through your personal dashboard.

3
Connect & Share

After attending events, connect with other attendees and share your experience through ratings and reviews to help improve future events.

Community

Built for local communities to thrive through shared experiences and meaningful connections.

Quality

Event approval system and feedback mechanisms ensure high-quality experiences for everyone.

Growth

Connect with like-minded people and grow your personal and professional network.

Built with Modern Technology

DMeet is built using cutting-edge web technologies to provide a fast, reliable, and secure experience:

  • Django + GeoDjango: Robust backend with spatial data support
  • PostgreSQL + PostGIS: Advanced spatial database capabilities
  • Bootstrap 5: Responsive, mobile-first design
  • Leaflet.js: Interactive maps with OpenStreetMap
  • Security First: CSRF protection, input validation, and user authentication

Privacy & Security

Your privacy and security are our top priorities:

  • Location data is only used for event discovery
  • Event approval system prevents spam and inappropriate content
  • Secure user authentication and password management
  • Connection limits prevent unwanted networking attempts
  • Feedback system maintains community standards
